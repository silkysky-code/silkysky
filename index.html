Company Overview

公司简介

Silk Route Navigators is a company specializing in bespoke high-end business tours, focused on providing entrepreneurs, investors, and industry leaders from Europe, the US, and other developed countries with deep market insights. We explore the technological innovation, advanced manufacturing, and business ecosystems of China’s Yangtze River Delta, leveraging close partnerships with leading companies, universities, and government entities to deliver unparalleled business tour experiences.

丝路领航是一家专注于提供高端定制化商务考察的公司，旨在为来自欧美及其他发达国家的企业家、投资人及行业领袖提供深入的市场洞察。我们深入考察中国长三角的科技创新、先进制造和商业生态系统，借助与领先企业、大学及政府机构的紧密合作，为客户提供无与伦比的商务考察体验。
